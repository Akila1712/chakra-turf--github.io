Chakra Turf is Rajapalayam's premier destination for cricket and football enthusiasts. Designed with international standards, our facility offers a premium synthetic turf surface that ensures excellent bounce, grip, and all-weather play. Whether you're looking to train, compete, or simply enjoy a game with friends, our turf delivers the ideal environment. Our infrastructure supports 5-a-side football, turf cricket matches, and friendly weekend events. With well-maintained facilities and vibrant surroundings, Chakra Turf brings energy and community together. Our arena is equipped with high-power floodlights for night play, CCTV surveillance for safety, and on-site staff for assistance. We also provide an online booking system with real-time slot updates, making it easier than ever to secure your preferred time. At Chakra Turf, we believe sports bring people together, boost health, and build character. Book your game today and be part of Rajapalayam’s fast-growing sports culture. Let the game begin!

PREMIUM TURF QUALITY

We use international-grade synthetic turf, regularly maintained for durability and safety. Players enjoy consistent bounce, smooth gameplay, and injury prevention—making every match enjoyable and professional.

Our smart booking system ensures up-to-date availability and instant slot reservations. With 90% accuracy in time slot display, we help avoid double bookings and ensure a smooth experience.

HIGH RETURN RATE

More than 80% of players come back weekly, showing their trust and love for Chakra Turf. We host regular events and offer attractive packages for teams, schools, and corporates.
